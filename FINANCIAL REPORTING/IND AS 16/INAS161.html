IND AS 16: PROPERTY PLANT AND EQUIPMENT

1. Definition:

a. Tangable asset

b. Held for use

c. Haveing life of more than 12 months

2. Scope of INDAS 16 Excludes

S NO PURTICULARS Covered Under
1 Non-Current Assets Held for Sale INDAS 105
2 Explonatory and Evaluation Assets linked to mining operations INDAS 106
2 Biological Assets for agericultureal operations (Except Bearer plants)* INDAS 41

* 2.1 Bearer plants Means: Thease are the living plants which bear agriculture produce and is not agericulture produce themselves
Eg: Coconut Tree, Mango Tree & All Fruit plantations

- They are excepted to bare the produce for more than 12 months (eg: Wheat grain plantation will bear the wheat in less than 12 Months and then will be choped off. Hence not a barer plant)

- There is a remote likehood of the plant itself being sold as a agericultureal produce except for incedental scrap sales.(eg: A banana & Mango Trees is held for the produce, Howerever, ultimately the tree may need to be cut down and sold. classification will be based on primary intention and hence it is a bearer plant)
