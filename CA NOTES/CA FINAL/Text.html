IND AS 16: PROPERTY PLANT AND EQUIPMENT

1. Definition:

a. Tangible asset

b. Held for use

c. Having life of more than 12 months

2. Scope of IND AS 16 Excludes

S NO PURTICULARS Covered Under
1 Non-Current Assets Held for Sale IND AS 105
2 Exploratory and Evaluation Assets linked to mining operations IND AS 106
3 Biological Assets for agricultural operations (Except Bearer plants)* IND AS 41

* 2.1 Bearer plants Means: These are the living plants which bear agricultural produce and are not agricultural produce themselves.
Eg: Coconut Tree, Mango Tree & All Fruit plantations.

- They are expected to bear the produce for more than 12 months (e.g., Wheat grain plantation will bear the wheat in less than 12 months and then will be chopped off. Hence not a bearer plant).

- There is a remote likelihood of the plant itself being sold as agricultural produce except for incidental scrap sales (e.g., A banana & Mango Trees is held for the produce. However, ultimately the tree may need to be cut down and sold. Classification will be based on primary intention and hence it is a bearer plant).
